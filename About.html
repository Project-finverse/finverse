From Scratch

💭 It started with a simple question: "Why don't schools teach us about money?"

As Class 12 students, we watched our seniors step into college and adulthood — clueless about SIPs, taxes, EMIs, and cyber safety. We saw families lose money to scams. We saw talented students confused about their first salary. And we knew something had to change.

So we — Saksham and Archit — two friends from Rudrapur, decided to build the solution ourselves. No shortcuts. No templates. Just pure passion, hours of research, and countless cups of chai. ☕

Every lesson was written to feel like a friend explaining, not a textbook lecturing. Every simulator was crafted with real Indian numbers. Every quiz was designed to make you think, not just memorize.

When we stood on the stage of CBSE Skillexpo 2026 and heard our name announced as winners — it wasn't just a trophy. It was proof that students can build for students. That dreams from a small town in Uttarakhand can reach the entire country. 🇮🇳

This is just the beginning. FinVerse is our promise — to make sure no student ever feels lost about money again. 💚

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is FinVerse free? +
Yes! FinVerse offers a completely free plan with access to all 5 Basics Modules, interactive simulators, quizzes, XP, and badges. Premium plans are available for advanced features like AI mentor, portfolio builder, and live trading simulator.
Who is FinVerse for? +
FinVerse is designed for teenagers and young adults (ages 15–21) looking to build financial literacy. Whether you're a school student, college fresher, or just starting your money journey, FinVerse makes learning finance simple and fun.
Do I get a certificate? +
Yes! Complete all 5 Basics modules and score at least 70% in the final assessment to unlock your official FinVerse Basics Certificate — a great addition to your resume and LinkedIn profile.
How was FinVerse built? +
FinVerse was built entirely from scratch by two passionate Class 12 students — Saksham Gupta and Archit Jain — from Rudrapur, Uttarakhand. Every lesson, quiz, and simulator was designed with real Indian context. This project won the CBSE Skillexpo 2026 Case Study Competition.
Are the simulators realistic? +
Absolutely! Our simulators use real formulas — SIP calculations use compound interest math, tax calculators use FY 2025-26 slabs, EMI calculators use standard banking formulas. All numbers reflect actual Indian financial products.
