💰 What Is Money?

Money is a universally accepted medium of exchange. Instead of exchanging goods directly through barter, money allows people to buy and sell efficiently. Today, money exists in many forms including cash, bank balances and digital payments.

💡 Key Insight

Money itself has little value unless people trust it. That trust comes from governments, banks and the economy.
